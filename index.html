Ch. 2  Who Am I?

I'm Spencer Wood!

A web developer, designer, and superhero enthusiast residing in the city of Louisville, Kentucky.

One Fateful Day...

...I was bitten by the development bug

Compelled, I began immersing myself in web development and design day and night, finding as many informational sources and courses that could teach me as I could possibly find. Then, dedicating my time to studying for hundreds of hours and completing numerous objective-based projects.

I quickly moved through the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while greatly enhancing my sense for creative graphic design and proficiency of the tools required in tandem.

The journey was daunting...

Fortunately, I loved every minute of it- it was the challenge I had been seeking and a way I could help others! Now I'm ready to swing into action as a Freelance Web Developer & Graphic Designer.
